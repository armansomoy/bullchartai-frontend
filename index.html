ZK proofs vs.
Validity Proofs

These terms are oftentimes used interchangeably in the industry since validity proofs are ZK proofs that don't hide parts of the computation or its results.

Validity ML

SNARK/STARK proofs of ML models where all computations are publicly visible to the verifier (). Any verifier can then prove the computational correctness of the ML models. while allowing an algorithm on a blockchain

ZKML

ZK proofs of ML models where computations are being hidden from the verifier (using the zero-knowledge property). The prover can prove the computational correctness of the ML models without revealing any further information.
